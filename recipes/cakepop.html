Whimsical Nose-Shaped Cakepops

Description

These delightful black wonders will make anyone smile. They don't suck - they are good.

Ingredients

  • Nose moulds
  • 400g Chocolate
  • 1/2 tsp Cacao Butter

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate in the microwave
  2. Mix in the cacao butter
  3. Pour into the moulds
  4. Refrigerate overnight
  5. Gently suck on these lollipops
