Tasteful but shy avocado toasts

Description

Share this lunch with your hipster friend. Makes 2 toasts

Ingredients

  • 2 toasted slices of bread
  • 1 Medium Avocado
  • Garnish you choose

Steps

  1. Spread half an avocado on each toast
  2. Lick some
  3. Spread the garnish as you please
  4. Ahoy! in the mouth!
